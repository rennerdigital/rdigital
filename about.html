Hi.

Renner here - nice to meet you. This is my journey.

I've been a software engineering enthusiast before ever knowing what it was - ever since I learned to type on a Commodore 64 back in grade 1. Pressing keys and watching letters and numbers appear on a screen sure did fascinate five year old me.

However, life took me in a different direction - briefly.

Fast forward 9 years, and I'm pursuing the pivot.
